Etch-A-Sketch

Usage:

Draw by hovering your mouse on the canvas grid and moving it around.

Black: This is the default pen colour.
Erase: This is used to erase individual squares. Hover over the square you would like to erase as if you were drawing on it.
Rainbow: Use the rainbow button to draw in a pen that colours each square in a random colour.
Resize Grid: Enter a number to choose how many columns and rows your grid will have. A higher number has the affect of making your pen thinner, a lower number does the opposite.
Toggle Grid: Removes the grid lines.
